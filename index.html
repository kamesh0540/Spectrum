About Spectrum
Spectrum has a Vision for Mental Wellness

At Spectrum, our mission is to create a better world for children by spreading awareness about mental health and providing early screening for common disorders. Our website offers a reliable tool for parents and caregivers to screen children aged 6-18 for mental health concerns. It's important to note that our screening tool is not a diagnosis, but it can provide valuable information to help children get the support they need. Join us in creating a brighter future for children's mental health.
